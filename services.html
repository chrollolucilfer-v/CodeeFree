What the register does

Everything you need to find, compare, and track free courses.

FreeCodeFinder is free to use, for every service below. No subscription, no paywall on the register itself — only individual courses may charge for their own certificates.

Core services
SVC · 01Free

Course discovery & search

Search across 12,400+ courses from 40+ universities and platforms with one query, instead of repeating the same search on every site.

  • Keyword and skill-based search with suggestions
  • Filter by university, platform, category, language, or level
  • Sort by popularity, rating, newest, or duration
SVC · 02Free

Side-by-side comparison

Line up courses from different sources to compare what actually differs — price, certificate type, duration, and difficulty.

  • Compare up to four courses at once
  • Clear free-vs-paid certificate labeling
  • Highlights of overlapping vs. unique skills covered
SVC · 03Free

Saved courses & learning lists

Bookmark courses as you browse and group them into personal lists — a semester plan, a certification track, or just a "maybe later" pile.

  • One-click save from any course card
  • Custom named learning lists
  • Access your saved list from any device once logged in
SVC · 04Free

Category & skill browsing

Browse by field — Web Development, AI, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and more — when you know the direction but not the exact course yet.

  • 13 core category pages
  • Course counts kept current with the register
  • Skill tags on every course listing
SVC · 05Free

Certificate transparency

Every listing states plainly whether a certificate is free, paid, or unavailable — so you're never surprised by a paywall after finishing a course.

  • Free-certificate and paid-certificate tags on every card
  • Featured certificate spotlights updated weekly
  • Last-updated date shown on every listing
SVC · 06Free

Weekly register digest

A once-a-week email with newly added free courses and certificates, sorted by category if you tell us what you're interested in.

  • One email per week, unsubscribe anytime
  • Optional category filtering
  • No sponsored placements mixed in
For universities & platforms
SVC · 07Partner

Get listed in the register

If you're a university or platform offering free courses, you can request to be added or updated in the register at no cost.

  • We verify course details before listing
  • Listings link directly to your original course page
  • Update requests handled through the contact page

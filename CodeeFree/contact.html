Quick answers

Is FreeCodeFinder actually free?

Yes — searching, filtering, saving courses, and the weekly digest are all free. Some individual courses charge for their certificate; we always label that clearly.

How do I suggest a course or source?

Pick "Suggest a course or source" in the form and include a link — we verify details before adding anything to the register.

A listing looks outdated. What do I do?

Select "Report an outdated or incorrect listing" and include the course name or a link so we can recheck it quickly.
